[ 2 eslatma ] U professional futbol tarixidagi eng ko'p mukofotlangan futbolchi bo'lib45 ta jamoaviy sovrinni, [ 3 eslatma ] , jumladan, o'n ikkitaKatta Beshlikligasi, to'rttaUEFA Chempionlar Ligasi, ikki martaAmerika Kubogiva bittaFIFA Jahon kubogi. MessiEvropaningeng ko'p(6), kalendar yilidagi eng ko'p gol (91), bitta klub uchun eng ko'p gol (672,"Barselona"), eng ko'pgolurgan(474),La Ligadagolurgan(36) vagolliuzatma(192),eng ko'pgolli uzatma(18) vagolli uzatma (32) bo'yicharekordlarga ega.o'yinlar(191) vaxalqaro gollar(112) va ikkinchi toifadagi ikkinchi o'rinda turadi.klub va mamlakat uchun 850 dan ortiq golurgan Born in Rosario, Argentina, Messi relocated to Spain to join Barcelona at age 13, and made his competitive debut at age 17 in October 2004. He gradually established himself as an integral player for the club, and during his first uninterrupted season at age 22 in 2008–09 he helped Barcelona achieve the first treble in Spanish football. This resulted in Messi winning the first of four consecutive Ballons d'Or, and by the 2011–12 season he would set La Liga and European records for most goals in a season and establish himself as Barcelona's all-time top scorer. The following two seasons, he finished second for the Ballon d'Or behind Cristiano Ronaldo, his perceived career rival. However, he regained his best form during the 2014–15 campaign, where he became the all-time top scorer in La Liga, led Barcelona to a historic second treble, and won a fifth Ballon d'Or in 2015. He assumed Barcelona's captaincy in 2018 and won a record sixth Ballon d'Or in 2019. During his overall tenure at Barcelona, Messi won a club-record 34 trophies, including ten La Liga titles and four Champions Leagues, among others. Financial difficulties at Barcelona led to Messi signing with French club Paris Saint-Germain in August 2021, where he would win the Ligue 1 title during both of his seasons there. He joined Major League Soccer club Inter Miami in July 2023. Argentinalik futbolchi Messi terma jamoaning barcha vaqtlardagi eng yaxshi to'purari va eng ko'p o'yin o'tkazgan futbolchisi hisoblanadi . Uning chap oyoqli driblingchi sifatidagi o'yin uslubi vatandoshi Diego Maradona bilan karyerasi davomida taqqoslangan , u Messini o'zining vorisi deb ta'riflagan . Yoshlar darajasida u 2005 yilgi FIFA yoshlar o'rtasidagi Jahon chempionati va 2008 yilgi Yozgi Olimpiya o'yinlarida oltin medalni qo'lga kiritdi . 2005 yildagi debyutidan so'ng Messi 2006 yilda jahon chempionatida o'ynagan va gol urgan eng yosh argentinalik bo'ldi . 2011 yilda sardorlikni qabul qilib, Argentinani 2014 yilgi FIFA Jahon kubogi , 2015 yilgi Amerika Kubogi va Kopa Amerika Sentenario musobaqalarida ketma-ket uchta finalga olib chiqdi , bularning barchasida mag'lub bo'lishdi. Dastlab 2016 yilda xalqaro nafaqaga chiqqanini e'lon qilgandan so'ng, u o'z mamlakatiga 2018 yilgi FIFA Jahon chempionatiga saralashda yordam berish uchun qaytib keldi , ular erta chiqishadi. Messi va milliy terma jamoa 2021 yilgi Amerika kubogida g'alaba qozonish orqali Argentinaning 28 yillik kubogini yengib chiqdi va bu unga o'sha yili ettinchi "Oltin to'p"ni qo'lga kiritishiga yordam berdi . Keyin u Argentinani 2022 yilgi Finalissima , shuningdek, 2022 yilgi FIFA Jahon chempionatida g'alaba qozondi , bu o'z mamlakatining uchinchi umumiy jahon chempionati va 36 yil ichida birinchi bo'ldi. Buning ortidan 2023 yilda rekord darajadagi sakkizinchi "Oltin to'p" va 2024 yilda Amerika Kubogida g'alaba qozonildi . Messi 2006-yildan beri Adidas sport kiyimlari kompaniyasini ma’qullab keladi. France Football ma’lumotlariga ko‘ra , u 2009-2014 yillar oralig‘ida olti yil ichida besh yil davomida dunyoning eng ko‘p maosh oluvchi futbolchisi bo‘lgan va 2019 va 2022 yillarda Forbes talqini bo‘yicha dunyoning eng ko‘p maosh oluvchi sportchisi bo‘lgan . 2011, 2012 va 2023. 2020 va 2023 yillarda u Laureus tomonidan yilning eng yaxshi jahon sportchisi deb topildi , uni qo'lga kiritgan birinchi jamoaviy sportchi. 2020-yilda Messi “Oltin to‘p”ni orzu qilgan jamoaga kiritildi va martaba daromadi 1 milliard dollardan oshgan ikkinchi futbolchi va ikkinchi jamoaviy sportchiga aylandi.
